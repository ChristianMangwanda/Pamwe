Pamwe · Home Screen Widget

Verse of the Day

One verse each morning, keyed to the calendar date. A tree of life stands at the center of the frame, its branches and roots framing the verse, so the widget feels quiet and rooted even though it never moves. Small, medium, and large, in light and dark.

SSmall · emblem + short form

At 170 pt there is no room for a long verse. The tree sits behind as a faint centered emblem and the app serves a pre-selected short line from the same day's entry, clamped to four lines with a trailing ellipsis if it still overflows.

MMedium · verse over the tree

The tree is centered behind the verse, which is set over it with a soft glow so it stays legible. The verse is clamped to three lines; anything longer truncates with an ellipsis before the reference, which is always pinned to the bottom.

LLarge · room to breathe

Full 20–30 word verse with an eyebrow and generous leading. The tree grows to full height, canopy above and roots below the verse, so it anchors the frame without competing with the text.

◐Light & dark

Light mode is early-morning cream with a warm glow top-left; dark mode is a deep, warm near-black with a rosy accent. The tree recolors from warm maroon to a soft rosy tone so it reads at a glance against either wallpaper.
